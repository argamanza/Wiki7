# Wiki7 Data Pipeline - Future Page Types

Ideas for additional wiki page types to add to the data pipeline.

## Planned

- **Competition pages** - Israeli Premier League season overview, State Cup, Toto Cup, Conference/Europa League
- **Season overview pages** - Main page per season linking squad, matches, transfers, stats
- **Player statistics leaderboards** - Top scorers, most appearances, assist leaders by season and all-time

## Wishlist

- **Coach/Manager pages** - Career history at Hapoel Beer Sheva, match record, trophies
- **Venue/Stadium pages** - Turner Stadium details, capacity, history
- **Derby/Rivalry pages** - Historical results vs Maccabi Tel Aviv, Beitar Jerusalem, Hapoel Tel Aviv
- **Historical records** - All-time top scorers, longest winning streaks, biggest wins/losses
- **Award pages** - Player of the Month, Season MVP, league awards
- **Youth academy pages** - U19 squad data, graduates who reached the first team
- **Club info page** - Club history, honors, ownership, badge evolution
- **Referee pages** - Referee assignments and stats per match
- **Attendance statistics** - Average attendance by season, highest/lowest attended matches
